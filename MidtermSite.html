Click the purple hearts for a fun fact about me!

❣My name is Peytan Ferris, and I’m going to tell you about my hobbies, so here are the things that make me, me!

HARLEY

This is my fur baby Harley, she is a Greyador which means she’s half Greyhound and half Black Lab. She is 3 years old and a very big part of my life, I say she’s one of my hobbies because playing and snuggling with her is one of my favorite parts of the day!

BTS

This is the KPOP group BTS, I have been following them since 2017! I collect a lot of their merchandise and albums and have gone to one of their concerts in 2019. Collecting their albums and photo cards is such a fun way to support them and their music truly brings me joy!❣

MUSICALS

Since I was little I’ve had a love for music and theatre. I am a big musical theatre nerd and have even seen a show on broadway! I also sing and am currently teaching myself how to play the piano!

TRAVEL

I love to travel! I have been to New York, Florida, Hawaii, Michigan, California, and I was supposed to go to Japan in May, but then Covid hit. I love going to see new places and learn new cultures, forever expanding my mind!❣
